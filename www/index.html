About

The company was started in the first quarter of 2018 by Ante Adamović, a younger software engineer from Zagreb, Croatia. At the time he already had a couple of active clients and great passion for software architecture. He started a company so he can eventually start sharing the knowledge he gathered throughout the years and help others. By 2019 he already started hiring more people to help him run the company, along with young people with great will to learn how to code, but were never given a chance, and has partnered with 2 startup companies to help them build and market their products. The company currently works with a number of clients from Europe and United States but plans to expand it's operations to other pieces of the globe in the upcoming future.
